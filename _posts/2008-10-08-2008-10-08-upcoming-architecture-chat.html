The Architecture Chat is Tomorrow - or probably "today" by the time your read this - Thursday 9th October, 11:30am, Garrisons, Sylvia Park.

  • JQuery shipping with Visual Studio .Net (only interesting because of the political implications - at least for those of us doing Monorail development - but a very positive sign none the less).
  • Mono gets a spot at PDC 2008 -about time I say too, it's good to see Mono getting the respect it deserves :)
  • NHibernate ProxyGenerators 1.0.0 Beta Released - great for those dealing with medium trust (see the intro for details).
  • Mass transit - definitely a "watch this space" project - they just hit milestone 0.4.
