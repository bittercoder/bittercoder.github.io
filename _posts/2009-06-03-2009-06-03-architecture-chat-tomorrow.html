Apologies for the last minute notice, been a crazy week!

Tomorrow is the Auckland Architecture Chat, some quick thoughts as to topics:

  • Google wave - worth watching the video if you haven't.
  • SilverUnit - unit testing for silverlight controls.
  • Automapper - fluently configured object-to-object mapper, this Dimecast provides a quick overview.
  • Linq2Sql changes for .Net 4.0.
  • NHibernate - Executable DML, no criteria support but pretty handy none the less, especially when targeting multiple databases.
  • Simone's .net ajax survey, some of the comments re: update panel etc. are interesting reading too.  It's not too late to take the survey here either.
  • Video for everyone, nice fallback approach to embedding video for all browsers.
  • Microsoft bing, "Decision engine" ?
  • Displaying eager load graphs in lightspeed designer - cool feature.
  • The Azure Services Platform Developer Challenge.


We'll be meeting at the usual time of 11:30am @ Garrisons, Sylvia Park, Tomorrow (Thursday 4th June 2009) - and the topics above are just there in case we run out of anything else to talk about (which is rare).

For more details on the location and write-ups of previous sessions you can consult the associated wiki.
